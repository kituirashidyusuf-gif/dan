Family Identity & Story

✨ Welcome to our digital hearth. The Rashid Kitui family began with Elena and David’s dream in a small town in Kampala. Today, we are a close-knit family brought together by shared moments, meaningful traditions, and a love for spending time with one another. Our core values are kindness, curiosity, and togetherness, and each member contributes something special to who we are. This space is where we celebrate everyday moments—from favorite recipes to memorable family trips. Thank you for being part of our story. ❤️
